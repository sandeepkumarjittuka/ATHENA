# Project Vision

ATHENA aims to become an institutional-grade AI-powered market intelligence platform that continuously monitors the Indian stock market, identifies high-probability intraday and scalping opportunities, explains every recommendation with transparent reasoning, and assists traders throughout the complete lifecycle of a trade.

ATHENA is designed to reduce information overload by transforming thousands of market events into a small number of high-quality, explainable opportunities.

---

# Mission Statement

Build an AI-powered decision support platform that helps traders make disciplined, evidence-based trading decisions through continuous market monitoring, technical analysis, news intelligence, risk assessment, and active trade management.

ATHENA does not replace trader judgment.

ATHENA enhances trader decision-making.

---

# Project Objectives

The primary objectives of ATHENA are:

* Monitor the entire NSE and BSE markets continuously.
* Detect high-quality intraday and scalping opportunities.
* Rank opportunities based on objective evidence.
* Explain every recommendation.
* Continuously monitor active trades.
* Recommend stop-loss adjustments.
* Recommend profit booking opportunities.
* Recommend exit timing.
* Generate comprehensive post-trade analysis.
* Improve decision quality through historical evaluation.

# Core Principles

1. Evidence over assumptions.

2. Transparency over black-box predictions.

3. Risk management before profit seeking.

4. Explain every recommendation.

5. "No Trade" is a valid recommendation.

6. Human remains the final decision-maker.

7. Every module must be independently testable.
